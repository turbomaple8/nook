Pricing questions

How much does a Nook room cost?

Rooms start from £215/week for Flex Basic, £345/week for Master and £355/week for Deluxe, subject to room availability. Every price is all-inclusive.

What is included in the weekly price?

A furnished room, WiFi, electricity, water and council tax, plus shared kitchen and living space. No agency fees and no separate bill setup.

How much deposit do I pay?

Two weeks' rent, protected in a government-approved deposit scheme. UK law allows up to five weeks — Nook asks for two.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The weekly price is the price you pay — no agency fees, no setup fees, no surprise service charges. If it is not in your contract, you are not billed for it.
