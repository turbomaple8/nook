Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your choices, keep you signed in, and (with your permission) measure how the site is used so we can make it better.

2. How we use them at Nook

We split cookies into three groups, and you decide which ones to allow:

Essential

Always on — needed for the site to work. We can't switch these off.

  • klaro — remembers your cookie choices for 365 days. Set by Klaro Consent Manager.

Analytics (only with your consent)

Helps us understand which pages and rooms visitors find interesting. Anonymous — we don't track you across other sites.

  • None active yet — analytics will be added in our next release. This page will be updated when they are.

Marketing (only with your consent)

Lets us measure whether ads we run on Meta or Google are reaching the right people. Only loaded if marketing campaigns are active.

  • None active yet — marketing pixels will be added when paid campaigns launch. This page will be updated when they are.

3. How to change your choices

You can change your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Click Manage cookie preferences to reopen the consent panel.
  • Or clear cookies for nookrent.com in your browser settings — the consent banner will appear again on your next visit.

4. Third-party cookies

When you accept Analytics or Marketing, those cookies are set by the relevant third-party service (Google or Meta). They have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy

5. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold, and to object to or restrict its processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@nookrent.com.

You can also lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

For any questions about cookies or privacy, please contact us at:
